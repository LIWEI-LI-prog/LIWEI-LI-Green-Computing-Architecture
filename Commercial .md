# Commercial Licensing Tiers

## Tier 1: Evaluation License
- **Access**: RTL evaluation code, simulation models
- **Duration**: 6-12 months
- **Fee**: €50,000 - €200,000 (one-time)
- **Use**: Internal evaluation only, no production rights

## Tier 2: Development License
- **Access**: Complete RTL, basic PDK, toolchain
- **Duration**: 2-3 years
- **Fee**: €1M - €3M (annual)
- **Use**: Prototype development, limited test chips

## Tier 3: Production License
- **Access**: Full stack (RTL/PDK/algorithms/support)
- **Term**: 10-year authorization
- **Fee**: €8M upfront + 3-5% royalty
- **Use**: Full commercial production

*所有报价为基准价格，战略合作伙伴可定制条款。*
